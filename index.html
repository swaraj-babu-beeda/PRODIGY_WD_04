Projects

Most recent work

Job portal Application

A Job Portal application using Java AWT, designed to do job searches, application submission and tracking effectively. The platform allows users to detect job opportunities in different fields based on their preferences and qualifications. Job seekers can search directly through the application, keep track of the submission and update from employers. The user -friendly interface increases accessibility, making the job search process more efficient. The aim of this project is to work to bridge job seekers and employers, who improve employment opportunities in different industries, and ensure a steady recruitment process, providing a spontaneous and organized platform for the job.

ElderLearn: Empowering Rural Elderly through Digital Education

ElderLearn is a completely web-based education platform, which was carefully developed using the entire MERN stack, along with the supreme goal of fully improving digital literacy in addition to supplying complete as well as accessible learning resources for the elderly in all rural areas. The platform's content is age-appropriate, easy to use, and features interactive components that match older adults' learning speeds. Video tutorials and quizzes are major features. Progress tracking guarantees a very engaging and effective learning experience. ElderLearn gives rural older adults important knowledge and skills to help close the digital divide, which promotes independence and improves their quality of life.

DigiSeniors: Bridging the Digital Divide for Rural Elderly

DigiSeniors is an online learning platform built using the MERN stack to facilitate digital literacy in the elderly rural population. The project reached more than 500 people in 10 villages with accessible learning content and easy navigation, along with age-relevant content. Key features are engaging video tutorials, quizzes, and tracking of progress, designed with the learning style of older persons in mind. DigiSeniors educates the elderly rural population in digital skills they can use daily to become self-reliant, socially inclusive, and increase digital service utilization, thus directly enriching the lives of those reached.
